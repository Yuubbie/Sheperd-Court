Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 1-2 business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our office directly at (555) 123-4567 during business hours.

Do you offer in-person consultations for prospective authors?

Yes, we do offer in-person consultations at our office by appointment. These consultations are particularly helpful for authors who want to discuss complex publishing projects or who prefer face-to-face meetings. Please contact us to schedule an appointment.

I have a specific publishing question not covered on your website. Who should I contact?

For specific publishing questions not addressed on our website, please email info@shepherdcourt.com with the subject line "Publishing Question" and we'll direct your inquiry to the appropriate team member who can best assist you.

Do you have representatives in other cities or countries?

While our main office is located in Brighton, Manchester, we have partner representatives in several major cities across the US and in the UK, Canada, and Australia. For international inquiries, please email international@shepherdcourt.com for the contact information of our representative nearest to you.
