About Us

Meet the team behind Shepherd Court Publishing

The Shepherd Court Story

Founded in 2010, Shepherd Court Publishing began with a simple mission: to empower authors by providing professional publishing services without the gatekeeping of traditional publishing houses.

Our founder, Victor Ashio, a former editor at one of the "Big Five" publishing houses, recognized that many talented authors were being overlooked by the traditional publishing model. She envisioned a company that would combine the quality standards of traditional publishing with the creative freedom and higher royalties of self-publishing.

Today, Shepherd Court has grown into a respected independent publisher with over 500 titles across multiple genres. We've helped both first-time authors and established writers bring their stories to readers worldwide, maintaining our commitment to quality, integrity, and author-centric publishing every step of the way.

🎯

Our Mission

To empower authors through professional publishing services that maintain the highest standards of quality while respecting creative vision and maximizing author control and profit.

We believe that great books deserve to be published, regardless of market trends or commercial considerations. Our goal is to help authors share their unique voices with the world.

🔭

Our Vision

To transform publishing by creating a model that combines the best aspects of traditional and self-publishing, leading to a more diverse, inclusive, and vibrant literary landscape.

We envision a future where authors have multiple viable paths to publication, and readers have access to a wider range of voices and perspectives.

Our Journey

The evolution of Shepherd Court Publishing

2010

The Beginning

Elizabeth Shepherd founded Shepherd Court Publishing with a vision to create a more author-friendly publishing model.

2012

First Bestseller

Our publication "The Silent Echo" by Jessica Winters reached the USA Today bestseller list, establishing our reputation for quality fiction.

2015

Digital Expansion

Launched our e-book division and expanded digital distribution channels, reaching readers worldwide.

2018

Academic Division

Established our academic publishing division, focusing on scholarly works and educational resources.

2020

10th Anniversary

Celebrated our 10th anniversary with over 300 published titles and launched our enhanced author services program.

2024

Global Reach

Expanded international distribution and translation services, bringing our authors' works to readers in over 30 countries.
